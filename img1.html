1. Athenian Tetradrachm


- Iconic "owl" silver coin bearing Athena's head on the obverse and her owl with olive sprig on the reverse.
-Minter from Laurion silver mines, it fueled Athenian trade and the city's Golden age.
-Its crisp style and bold eyes make it a numismatic masterpieve.
-It was known for its high weight standard and consistent silver cotent.
-Served as a trusted International currency in ancient world.
